Who We Are

The International Justice Organization is a global humanitarian movement that seeks to defend justice and human dignity, and to expose war crimes and violations committed by occupying or oppressive forces. We aim to raise global awareness and activate legal pathways to hold violators accountable.

Our Vision

A free and just world where the oppressed are supported, war crimes are exposed, and the people of Gaza and all occupied lands attain peace, justice, and dignity.

Our Mission

We strive to create a platform that unites the voices of justice from across the globe, spreading the truth, documenting crimes, and pushing for international trials for war criminals. Through solidarity and awareness, we move towards true justice and the restoration of human rights.

Core Values

  • Justice: We seek fair accountability for all crimes against humanity.
  • Transparency: All of our messages and actions are public and clear.
  • Unity: We believe in uniting people regardless of nationality, race, or religion in defense of justice.
  • Courage: We speak the truth and stand strong in the face of injustice.
  • Support for Palestine: Palestine is the core of our cause, and Gaza is the spark of justice in our movement.
